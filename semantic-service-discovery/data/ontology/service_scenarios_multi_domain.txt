============================================================
DATASET DESIGN NOTES
============================================================

- The expanded dataset contains 300 services and 180 user queries.
- The dataset includes two domains: Travel and E-commerce.
- Each domain contains 150 services and 90 queries.
- The service distribution is designed to cover both broad service categories and fine-grained subconcepts.
- Exact, plugin, partial, and irrelevant relevance labels are used for evaluation.
- Exact, plugin, and partial matches are considered relevant during metric calculation.
- The dataset is a controlled benchmark dataset constructed for experimental evaluation.
- Duplicate service names and duplicate query texts should be checked during validation.
- The taxonomy hierarchy is used to support taxonomy-based and taxonomy-distance based semantic matching.
