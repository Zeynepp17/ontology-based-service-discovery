SERVICE SCENARIO PLAN

Domain: Travel

1. AccommodationService - 16 services
   Purpose: Services related to hotels, hostels, apartments, villas, resorts, camping and general accommodation search/reservation.

2. TransportationService - 15 services
   Purpose: Services related to flight, train, bus, taxi, airport transfer, car rental, bike rental and route-based transportation.

3. TourismService - 9 services
   Purpose: Services related to tour guides, museums, historical places, city tours, events, restaurants and tourist attractions.

4. PlanningService - 8 services
   Purpose: Services related to trip planning, budget planning, route optimization, vacation recommendation and schedule planning.

5. DocumentationService - 4 services
   Purpose: Services related to visa applications, passport renewal, embassy appointments and travel document verification.

6. InsuranceAndPaymentService - 5 services
   Purpose: Services related to travel insurance, booking cancellation, refunds, currency exchange and international payments.

7. SafetyAndWeatherService - 3 services
   Purpose: Services related to weather forecasts, travel alerts, safe destination recommendations and risk assessment.

Total planned services: 60


NOTES

- Service distribution was intentionally designed in a non-uniform way to better reflect realistic travel ecosystems.

- Accommodation and Transportation services were given higher representation because they are the most frequently used service categories in travel-related applications.

- Documentation and Safety services were kept smaller due to their more limited and specialized usage scenarios.

- QoS parameters (response time, cost score, reliability) will later be added to distinguish semantically similar services.
