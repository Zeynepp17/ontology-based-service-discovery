NOTES

- This taxonomy structure is used to represent semantic relationships between service concepts.
- The revised study uses this structure as a taxonomy-based semantic hierarchy rather than a complete OWL/RDF/SPARQL-based ontology infrastructure.
- Parent-child concept relationships are used for taxonomy-based matching.
- Concept distance in this hierarchy is used for taxonomy-distance based semantic similarity.
- Travel and E-commerce were selected to evaluate the framework across multiple service domains.
